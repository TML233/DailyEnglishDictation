*Don't ever(1)* let somebody tell you, you can't do something.

1. S/N/L 音与 d/t/th 音相接，后者音可以被取消。
    * `You and I` -> `You an-ni`
    * `International` -> `In-nernational`
    * `Don't ever` -> `Don-never`
    * `You can't do` -> `You can- do` can后有停顿
